about us

S.B.V.R. Degree College was started in the year 1979. Since its inception the college has gone from strength to strength. Now it is one of the “oldest college” in our District with students strength of about 1000. To keep pace with sweeping changes that are taking place in the higher education sphere, our college acquired and added many Modern Infrastructural facilities and latest Technical I.T. related facilities.

‘‘A Quality Education has the power to transform societies in a single generation, by providing knowledge, necessary skills and confidence to the students to reach their full potential’’ The S.B.V.R. Degree College was established in 1979 in the Badvel Town with a noble Vision and clear perception about the dynamic change to be brought in the field of education. The College is fruitfully put up a journey of 44 long years under the Badvel & Sidhout Higher Education Committee under which the Academy established 8 educational institutions to its credit and with 2 PG Courses accompanied with S.B.V.R. Degree College.

Our Vision is, To impart quality education by inculcating value based education, Imparting Skill Oriented knowledge according to the changing global needs. To refresh the students and staff with good resources by mobilizing various strengths by effective use of curriculum and by promoting the use of technology. To develop dynamic mechanism in the college for spreading its extension activities and other services.
